📱 Publishing Steps

1 Open LinkedIn: Go to linkedin.com or open the mobile app
2 Create Post: Click "Start a post"
3 Copy Content: Copy the ready text and paste it into the text box
4 Add Image: Add your professional photo or portfolio screenshot (optional)
5 Select Audience: Choose "Anyone" for wider reach
6 Publish: Review the text and click "Post"
